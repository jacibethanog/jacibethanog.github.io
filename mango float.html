Mango Float

Ingredients:

3-4 ripe mangoes, sliced

2 cups heavy cream or all-purpose cream, chilled

1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 pack (200g) graham crackers


Instructions:

1.In a mixing bowl, whip the chilled heavy cream until it forms stiff peaks. You can use an electric mixer or a whisk for this step.

2.Gradually add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract to the whipped cream. Gently fold the mixture until well combined. Be careful not to overmix to maintain the fluffy texture.

3.In a rectangular glass dish or any container of your choice, arrange a single layer of graham crackers at the bottom. You may need to break some crackers to fit the edges.

4.Spread a layer of the whipped cream mixture over the graham crackers, making sure to cover them evenly.

5.Place a layer of sliced mangoes on top of the cream mixture, covering the entire surface.

6.Repeat the layers: graham crackers, cream mixture, and mangoes until you reach the desired height or run out of ingredients. The top layer should be the cream mixture.

7.Cover the dish with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld and the graham crackers to soften.

8.Before serving, you can optionally garnish the Mango Float with additional mango slices or crushed graham crackers for added texture.

9.Slice and serve the Mango Float chilled. Enjoy!
