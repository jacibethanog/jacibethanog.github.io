Leche Plan

Ingredients:

10 egg yolks

1 can (12 oz) condensed milk

1 can (14 oz) evaporated milk

1 cup granulated sugar

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

3/4 cup granulated sugar

1/4 cup water


Instructions:

1.Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)

2.Prepare the caramel: In a saucepan,

combine the 3/4 cup sugar and water over

medium heat.Stir until the sugar is dissolved,

then let it cook without stirring until it turns amber in color, about 8-10 minutes.

Remove from heat and carefully pour the caramel into individual llaneras

(caramel molds), tilting the molds to evenly coat the bottom. Set aside to cool and harden.

3.In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract until well combined.

4.Strain the egg mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any lumps.

5.Pour the strained mixture into the prepared llaneras with caramel, filling them almost to the top.

6.Cover each llanera tightly with aluminum foil.

7.Place the llaneras in a baking pan and create a water bath by pouring hot water into the pan until it reaches halfway up the sides of the llaneras.

8.Carefully transfer the baking pan to the preheated oven and bake for about 45-50 minutes, or until the flan is set but still jiggles slightly in the center.

9.Remove the llaneras from the water bath and let them cool to room temperature.

10.Once cooled, refrigerate the flan for at least 4 hours or overnight to chill and set completely.

11.To serve, run a knife around the edges of each llanera to loosen the flan. Place a serving plate on top of the llanera and invert it to release the flan onto the plate, allowing the caramel to drizzle over the top.

12.Slice and enjoy your delicious leche flan!
