Halo-halo

Ingredients:

1.Crushed or shaved ice

2.Sweetened beans (red beans, chickpeas, or mung beans)

3.Sweetened fruits (such as langka or jackfruit, macapuno or sweetened coconut sport, and nata de coco or coconut jelly)

4.Sweetened kaong (sugar palm fruit)

5.Sweetened saba bananas (optional)

6.Sweetened sweet potatoes (optional)

7.Sweetened corn kernels (optional)

8.eche flan (caramel custard), sliced into cubes

9.Ube halaya (purple yam jam)

10.Pinipig (toasted rice flakes)

11.vaporated milk or condensed milk

12.Ice cream (optional)

13.Leche flan or ube halaya for garnish (optional)


Instructions:

1.Prepare the sweetened beans and fruits by draining them if canned. You can also cook fresh beans and fruits in sugar syrup until tender and sweetened.

2.Assemble all the ingredients in separate bowls or containers for easy access.

3.In a tall glass or bowl, layer the halo-halo starting with crushed or shaved ice at the bottom.

4.Add a variety of sweetened beans, fruits, and other ingredients on top of the ice. You can customize the ingredients according to your preference.

5.Drizzle a generous amount of evaporated milk or condensed milk over the halo-halo.

6.Top the halo-halo with leche flan or ube halaya for added sweetness and flavor.

7.Optional: Add a scoop of your favorite ice cream on top of the halo-halo for extra creaminess.

8.Garnish the halo-halo with pinipig for crunch and texture.

9.Serve the halo-halo immediately and mix all the ingredients together before enjoying.

10.Enjoy the refreshing and delightful flavors of halo-halo, perfect for hot summer days or any occasion!
