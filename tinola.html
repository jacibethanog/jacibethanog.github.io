Tinola

Ingredients:

1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces

1 green papaya or chayote, peeled and cut into wedges

2-3 thumb-sized ginger pieces, sliced thinly

1 medium-sized onion, chopped

3-4 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons cooking oil

1 bunch chili leaves (or spinach or malunggay leaves)

2-3 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)

6 cups water

Salt and pepper to taste


Instructions:

1.n a large pot, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Saute garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.

2.Add the chicken pieces to the pot and cook until they start to brown slightly.

3.Pour water into the pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and let it simmer until the chicken is almost tender, about 20-25 minutes.

4.Once the chicken is almost tender, add the green papaya or chayote wedges to the pot. Continue simmering until the vegetables are cooked but still slightly firm.

5.Season the soup with fish sauce, salt, and pepper according to taste. Adjust seasoning if needed.

6.Add the chili leaves (or spinach or malunggay leaves) to the pot and simmer for another 1-2 minutes until wilted.

7.Serve the Tinola hot in bowls with steamed rice on the side. Enjoy your comforting and flavorful Filipino Tinola soup!
