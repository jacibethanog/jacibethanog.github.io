Buko salad

Ingredients:

3 cups young coconut (buko), shredded or strips

1 cup canned fruit cocktail, drained

1 cup nata de coco (coconut jelly), drained

1 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit), drained

1 cup sweetened condensed milk

1 cup heavy cream or all-purpose cream, chilled

1 cup cheese, cubed (optional)

1 cup mini marshmallows (optional)

Ice cubes (optional)


Instructions:

1.In a large mixing bowl, combine the young coconut strips, canned fruit cocktail, nata de coco, and kaong. Mix well to distribute the fruits evenly.

2.In a separate bowl, whip the chilled heavy cream or all-purpose cream until it forms stiff peaks.

3.Gradually pour in the sweetened condensed milk while continuously whisking the whipped cream until well combined.

4.Add the whipped cream mixture to the bowl of fruits. Gently fold the cream into the fruits until everything is well coated.

5.If desired, add the cubed cheese and mini marshmallows to the mixture and gently fold them in.

6.Transfer the buko salad to a serving dish or individual dessert cups.

7.Chill the buko salad in the refrigerator for at least an hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld and the dessert to set.

8.Serve the buko salad cold, either on its own or with additional ice cubes for extra refreshment.
