Buko pandan

Ingredients:

2 cups young coconut (buko), shredded

1 pack (25g) green gulaman (gelatin) powder

4 cups water

1 pack (200ml) all-purpose cream

1 can (300ml) condensed milk

1 teaspoon pandan extract

1 cup small tapioca pearls (optional)


Instructions:

1.Prepare the Gulaman (Gelatin)

In a saucepan, dissolve the green gulaman powder in 4 cups of water. Stir until the powder is fully dissolved.

Bring the mixture to a boil while continuously stirring.

Once boiling, add 1 cup of sugar and stir until fully dissolved.

Pour the gulaman mixture into a rectangular container or mold. Let it cool and set aside until firm. Once set, cut the gulaman into cubes.

2.Prepare the Buko Pandan Mixture:

In a large bowl, combine the shredded young coconut (buko) and cooked tapioca pearls (if using).

Add the all-purpose cream, condensed milk, and pandan extract. Mix until well combined.

Gently fold in the cubed gulaman until evenly distributed throughout the mixture.

3.Chill and Serve:

Transfer the Buko Pandan mixture into a serving bowl or individual cups.

Refrigerate for at least an hour to chill before serving.

Serve cold and enjoy your delicious Buko Pandan dessert!
