Dinakdakan

Ingredients:

1 lb pork face (cheeks), ears, and/or liver, boiled and grilled

1 medium-sized red onion, finely chopped

2-3 pieces green chili peppers (siling pangsigang), chopped

3 tablespoons calamansi juice or lemon juice

3 tablespoons vinegar

3 tablespoons mayonnaise

Salt and pepper to taste

2 tablespoons vegetable oil


Instructions:

1.Boil the pork parts in water with salt until tender. Drain and grill over hot charcoal until lightly charred. Let them cool, then chop into small pieces.

2.In a mixing bowl, combine the chopped grilled pork parts, chopped onions, and chopped green chili peppers.

3.In a separate bowl, mix together calamansi juice, vinegar, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.

4.Pour the dressing over the pork mixture and toss until well combined. Adjust seasoning according to taste.

5.Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Once hot, add the pork mixture and cook for about 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through.

6.Transfer the cooked Dinakdakan to a serving plate and garnish with additional chopped onions and chili peppers if desired.

7.Serve hot with steamed rice or as a pulutan (appetizer) with your favorite beverage. Enjoy your delicious Dinakdakan!
