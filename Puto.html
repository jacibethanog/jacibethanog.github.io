Puto

Ingredients:

2 cups rice flour

1 cup granulated sugar

2 tablespoons baking powder

1/4 teaspoon salt

1 1/2 cups water

1/2 cup evaporated milk

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Grated cheese or salted egg (optional toppings)


Instructions:

1.In a large mixing bowl, combine the rice flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well to ensure that all ingredients are evenly distributed.

2.Add the water, evaporated milk, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth and well combined. The batter should have a pourable consistency similar to pancake batter.

3.Prepare your steamer by filling it with water and bringing it to a boil

4.While waiting for the steamer to boil, prepare your puto molds by greasing them lightly with oil or lining them with puto wrappers.

5.Once the steamer is ready, pour the puto batter into the prepared molds, filling each mold about three-quarters full.

6.If desired, sprinkle grated cheese or place slices of salted egg on top of each puto.

7.Arrange the filled molds in the steamer basket, making sure there's enough space between each mold to allow steam to circulate.

8.Steam the puto over medium heat for about 10-15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

9.Once cooked, remove the puto from the steamer and allow them to cool for a few minutes before removing them from the molds.

10.Serve the puto warm as a snack or dessert, and enjoy!
